=========================================
COMMON TROUBLESHOOTING
=========================================
Issue: Inverter displays a blinking Red Light.
Solution: This indicates a grid connection loss. The user should check their home's main electrical breaker. If the breaker is on, contact your local utility company.

Issue: Error Code E-404 on the Apollo Inverter screen.
Solution: The inverter has lost Wi-Fi connection. The customer needs to restart their home router and use the SolarTech mobile app to reconnect the inverter to the network.

Issue: Luna Battery is not charging during the day.
Solution: Verify that the solar panels are free of debris or heavy snow. If clear, force-restart the battery by holding the green power button for 10 seconds.
